My Projects

Right now I am working on my final thesis for the physics degree. The title is: Neural networks solutions for the quantum many-body problem. Basically, I am making use of a library named NetKet (https://www.netket.org/) to try to make a simple NN structure, like a RBM or a FFNN, learn the fundamental state of a given many-body system. The plan is to study a couple of different hamiltonians and check how these ML methods applied to physics can be useful and what can we expect from them.
